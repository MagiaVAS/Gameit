#Operations

Operations in gameit can be divided into following types:

   - Logical
   - Comparative
   - Arithmatic  


## Logical operations

   - `or` : a logical OR
   - `and` : a logical and

## Comparative operations

   - `<=>` : smaller, equal and larger
   - `!=` : not equal

## Arithmatic operations

   - `+` : plus
   - `-` : minus
   - `*` : multiply
   - `/` : divide 
   - `in` : check the membership
